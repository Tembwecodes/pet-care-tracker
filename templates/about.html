About Pet Care Tracker

Pet Care Tracker is a comprehensive application designed to help pet owners manage all aspects of their pet's care.

This application was created as a project for General Assembly's Software Engineering course.

Features include:

  • Track pet profiles with all essential information
  • Record medical history and vaccination records
  • Manage feeding schedules and medications
  • Monitor training progress
  • Create a photo gallery of your pets
